## 📌 Introduction
The Student Marketplace Platform is a web-based application that allows students to buy, sell, and exchange items such as books, electronics, and study materials within their campus.

## 🎯 Objective
- To provide a platform for students to sell and buy products
- To reduce waste by reusing items
- To create a trusted student community

## ❗ Problem Statement
Students face difficulty in finding affordable second-hand items and selling unused products within a trusted environment.

## 💡 Proposed Solution
A centralized platform where students can:
- Register and login
- Add products for sale
- Search and buy products
- Contact sellers

## 🧩 Modules
- User Module
- Product Module
- Search Module
- Admin Module

## 🛠️ Technology Stack
- Frontend: React.js
- Backend: Node.js, Express
- Database: MongoDB

## 🚀 Future Scope
- Online payment integration
